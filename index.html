How to make changes after your first version
✦
Tell it what to change in plain English. You do not need to know how to code. Just describe what you want differently. For example: "Make the hero headline bigger and change the button colour to dark green" or "Move the testimonials section above the pricing section."
✦
Be specific about one thing at a time. The AI works best when you give it one clear change per message rather than a long list. Change the copy first, then adjust the design, then fix the layout.
✦
To update your copy, paste in your real text and say: "Replace the placeholder text in the hero section with this:" followed by your actual headline and description.
✦
To add something new, describe it clearly. For example: "Add a two-column section below the hero with a photo on the left and a short paragraph about me on the right."
✦
If something looks wrong on mobile, say: "Fix the mobile layout — the hero text is overflowing on small screens." The AI will sort it.
